  • Create Title & Subtitle

  • To create a title and subtitle you must take into account the following portion of code

  • config.yml

                        
                        1 Enabled: true  
                        2 Title: &b&lWelcome %player%!
                        3 SubTitle: &b&lThanks for enter to the server

  • It must be taken into account that the Title and Subtitle support PlaceholderAPI

    The permission to see the title and subtitle upon entering is: je.title - je.*
